Who we are

Lopsat Services LTD is a logistics services company headquartered in Juba, South Sudan. We offer a broad portfolio of services including air freight services, road transportation services, roadways cargo services, sea cargo services, air freight logistics, customs clearance services, port handling services, and warehousing services.

Our expertise also includes comprehensive and tailor-made solutions to meet the relocation needs of corporate and residential clients. We are known for reliable cargo handling, freight logistics expertise, and hassle-free export and import clearance at Nimule border and Juba International Airport.

With an extensive logistics and communications network, we create cost-effective solutions tailored to each client. Across South Sudan and international lanes, our reputation is built on timeliness, flexibility, swiftness, safety, and reliability.

Today, our presence spans both domestic and overseas markets through strong international links. This growth is driven by well-planned services, defined modes of operation, a resourceful network, talented people, and strong business ethics.
