Air freight forwarding

Lopsat Services Ltd provides a highly efficient network for freight forwarding and domestic air freight. It delivers cargo across major locations of the country the very next day during business hours. With a wide range of time-defined and specialist air cargo services, supported by preferred carriers, you get flexibility between standardized connections with fixed schedules. Logistics experts evaluate your freight size and budget to assist with the most appropriate, cost-effective movement.

Road freight forwarding

We offer a variety of customized and cost-effective solutions for transportation by road. Freight can be transported to locations across South Sudan. Air cargo service

International freight forwarder
